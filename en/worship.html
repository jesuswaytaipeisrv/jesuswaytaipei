WayWorship

Conveying God's heart through original music

Founded in 2015, WayWorship is a music collective that conveys God's heart through original composition. With sincere and authentic lives, we have crafted numerous worship songs, hoping that more people will find satisfaction, joy, and comfort through this music — and connect their lives to God.

In 2021, their original song "Walking Into Your Promise" stood out from over 2,000 submissions to be nominated in GoodTV's "Global Worship Song Creation Competition," ultimately receiving 5th place in the Online Popularity Award with 9,529 votes.

To this day, we continue to collaborate with various groups and even reach across borders — all to keep spreading the sound of praise rising from every people group and every generation.
