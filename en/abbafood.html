ABBAFOOD Workplace Book Club

Full stomach, full heart

You've heard of Uber Eats and food delivery apps — but have you heard of ABBAFOOD?

This is something special we do during the lunch break in office buildings: we walk into the workplace during the short one-hour midday break, and deliver a surprise delicious lunch — along with nourishment for the soul: worship songs, good books, authentic workplace life stories, group discussion, and a Q&A.

We jokingly call ourselves the third-largest food delivery platform — because after that one hour, your stomach is full and your heart is satisfied too.

There's no barrier to entry and nothing to prepare. Come as you are — busy, tired, or wondering about the direction of your life. In a relaxed atmosphere, through a single shared story or a moment of honest conversation, you might just find renewed strength and clarity.

ABBAFOOD is more than a lunch gathering — it's a place where people in the workplace can slowly come to know God and come to know themselves.
